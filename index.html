How question parsing works

The parser looks for the common exam-dump pattern:

1. Question text here?
A. Choice one
B. Choice two
C. Choice three
D. Choice four
Answer: D
Explanation: optional text...

Multi-answer questions ("Answer: A,B,G") become checkbox questions automatically. Anything that doesn't match this pattern (e.g. drag-and-drop / hotspot items) is skipped and listed separately. All parsing happens locally in your browser — no file is uploaded anywhere.

If a PDF has no selectable text (scanned pages or outlined fonts), each page is rendered and read with OCR (Tesseract.js) — slower, and accuracy depends on scan quality.
